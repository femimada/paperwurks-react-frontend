Lease Agreement - 123 Sample St.

Lease Agreement

This Lease Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into this 1st day of January, 2024, by and between Landlord Name ("Landlord") and Tenant Name ("Tenant").

1. Property

Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, the premises located at 123 Sample Street, London, UK, together with all fixtures, fittings, and appurtenances (the "Premises").

2. Term

The term of this lease shall be for a period of twelve (12) months, commencing on February 1, 2024, and ending on January 31, 2025. Potential Risk: The short lease term may not be suitable for all lenders.

3. Rent

Tenant shall pay to Landlord as rent for the Premises the sum of £2,000 per month, due and payable in advance on the first day of each month. Key Clause: Rent amount and due date.

4. Security Deposit

Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of £2,000 as a security deposit. Potential Risk: The security deposit is equal to one month's rent, which may be insufficient to cover potential damages.

5. Use of Premises

The Premises shall be used and occupied by Tenant and Tenant's immediate family, consisting of 2 persons, exclusively, as a private single-family dwelling, and no part of the Premises shall be used at any time during the term of this Agreement by Tenant for the purpose of carrying on any business, profession, or trade of any kind, or for any purpose other than as a private single-family dwelling.

6. Maintenance and Repairs

Landlord shall be responsible for all major repairs and maintenance of the Premises, not caused by Tenant's misuse or neglect. Tenant shall be responsible for minor repairs and keeping the property in a clean and sanitary condition.
