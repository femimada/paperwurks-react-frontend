Your journey to a smoother property sale starts here. Let's get your property ready for the market.

Your Role in the Process

1. Complete Your Property Details

Provide accurate information about your property, including its history, features, and any known issues. Think of it as your property's CV.

2. Upload Necessary Documents

Securely upload documents like title deeds, energy performance certificates (EPC), and any guarantees. We'll guide you on what's needed.

3. Review and Approve the Pack

Carefully review the pre-sale pack we compile to ensure all information is correct. You're in full control to make amendments.

4. Share with Potential Buyers

Once approved, you can easily share the pack with estate agents and potential buyers, giving them all the information they need upfront for a faster, more transparent sale.
